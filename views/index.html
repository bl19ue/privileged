    Features
    what to expect
    sport clubs
    Create an awesome website
    Make your website stand out by choosing your own colour scheme and adding your club logo. No coding is required - just pick one of our beautiful designs and we do the rest. Also, site content can be updated in seconds, anytime, anywhere with any device. Keep your club community informed with news updates, match reports, photos, ...
    sport clubs
    Organize all your teams within your sport club
    Klublr can handle multiple teams within your club. You can assign team managers and club officials to a specific team and allow them to manage memberships, member availability and online payments. Next to that, you can keep track of the team's season (matches, reports, ranking, ...) and improve communication with simple notifications and teamtalks.
    sport clubs
    Increase your sport club's earnings
    We enable your club to earn some extra money. How? Well, for starters, you'll get your own club shop allowing you to sell merchandise. But that's not all: get more sponsors by allowing them to advertise on your website. Or simply activate our internal advertising system and receive monthly payouts by showing relevant advertisments in predefined areas on your website.
    sport clubs
    individuals
    Discover your sports community
    Finding new club members or a fitting sport club can be real hassle. Klublr Market helps sport clubs and individual sportmen, women & staff with this. We allow them to engage with each other by listing open vacancies at sport clubs as well as résumés of individuals looking for a sport club.
    individuals
    Extend your network by making an impression
    Create an awesome profile wich automatically updates with your match results and stats. Share articles, photos and videos and keep your community informed on your career. Find and connect with other sportsmen & women, get to know each other and share unique experiences. Who knows who or what may cross your path in time?
    Roadmap
    when to expect
    nov
    2015
    We've launched the landing page you're currently exploring. This page has multiple purposes. First of all, we want to make our target market aware of our product and inform them about the key features and benefits of Klublr. Furthermore, we want to give them the opportunity to get early access once we launch Klublr. In other words, pre-registrations are open!
    jan
    2016
    We aim to launch a beta version of klublr early 2016. This version, however, will not yet contain all features as mentioned above. We'll start with a few basic features such as the customizable website and its content, memberships, agenda, teamtalks,... We focus on getting these figured out completely, with the help of your valuable feedback, before moving on to the more sophisticated features.
    jul
    2016
    During the first months of 2016 we'll focus on improving the launched features of our beta, but also work on getting some new awesome features live. You can expect features as match reports, member availabilty, custom pages for the website, sponsor management, ... We also plan on rolling out Klublr Market, enabling sport clubs to find new members and individuals to get noticed in their search for a sport club.
    dec
    2016
    Payments, payments, payments. By the end of 2016 we will be implementing online payments. Sport clubs will get their own club shop allowing them to sell merchandise and they will be able to collect payments from their members for memberships, events, ... Also, we'll introduce our internal advertising system allowing sport clubs to show relevant advertisments in predefined areas on their website and receive monthly payouts.
    ...
    Who knows what the future brings. We'll constantly be brainstorming about new features and how to improve existing features. Your feedback will be most valuable in every stage of the development. The opportunities are endless...
